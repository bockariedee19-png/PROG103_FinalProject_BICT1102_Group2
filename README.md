SDG 8: Sales Management System
Project Title

Sales Management System for Decent Work and Economic Growth (SDG 8)

Description

The Sales Management System is a Python-based application developed to support the achievement of United Nations Sustainable Development Goal (SDG) 8: Decent Work and Economic Growth. The system helps businesses manage sales transactions, monitor product performance, track revenue, and improve operational efficiency.

By digitizing sales processes, the system contributes to business growth, increased productivity, and better decision-making, which are key objectives of SDG 8.

Objectives

Record and manage sales transactions efficiently.

Monitor product inventory and sales performance.

Generate sales reports for business analysis.

Improve productivity through automation.

Support economic growth by helping businesses make informed decisions.
